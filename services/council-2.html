Covid-19: Precautions From Mayor

  • Milton
  • Monday, May 06, 2024

Rider alert Milton Transit wants your input on a draft plan to improve transit for local residents, visitors, workers, and students. The Milton Transit Five-Year Service Plan reviewed services and will recommend transit changes within the Town's larger transportation network. After initial public input and a review of existing services, the draft service plan to address local travel needs from 2024-2029 is now open for comment. The survey asks for input on the proposed network and service levels compared to the current offerings. It also asks demographic questions to learn more about riders and non-riders. The survey is available online and in a fillable PDF, which can be submitted online as well as by email, mail or in person at Town Hall. Find all survey details at www.letstalkmilton.ca/milton-transit-five-year-service-plan
